    Common signs

    • Enlarged pores
    • Excessive oil production
    • Shiny appearance
    • Prone to breakouts, blackheads, and whiteheads

    Treatment goals

    • Remove excess oil and dirt with gentle cleansing
    • Unclog pores through exfoliation
    • Avoid harsh, drying products
