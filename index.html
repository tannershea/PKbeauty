Before your Appointment

  1. 01 Please arrive 5-10 minutes early to allow time for check-in and consultation.
  2. 02 Stay hydrated and avoid excessive sun exposure before your appointment.
  3. 03 Avoid wearing makeup to your appointment if you're receiving a facial treatment.
  4. 04 Let me know in advance if you're pregnant, on medications, or have any skin concerns.
  5. 05 Refrain from using retinol, AHAs, or exfoliants 48 hours before your service.
  6. 06 For waxing services, make sure hair is at least 1/4 inch long for best results.

Booking Policies

Appointments

Appointments must be scheduled at least 5 days in advance to ensure availability. Same-day or next-day bookings are not guaranteed.

Payment

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. The remaining balance is due at the time of your appointment.

Late policy

A 10-minute grace period is allowed for all appointments. After 10 minutes, your appointment may be shortened or canceled depending on availability.

Guests

For safety and professionalism, no extra guests are allowed during your appointment — unless approved beforehand.

Cancellation

You must cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations will result in a fee or forfeiture of your deposit.
