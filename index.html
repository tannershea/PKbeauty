Before your Appointment

  1. 01 Please arrive 5-10 minutes early to allow time for check-in and consultation.
  2. 02 Stay hydrated and avoid excessive sun exposure before your appointment.
  3. 03 Avoid wearing makeup to your appointment if you're receiving a facial treatment.
  4. 04 Let me know in advance if you're pregnant, on medications, or have any skin concerns.
  5. 05 Refrain from using retinol, AHAs, or exfoliants 48 hours before your service.
  6. 06 For waxing services, make sure hair is at least 1/4 inch long for best results.
